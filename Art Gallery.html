A Few Words About Me

My name is ASEEM JOSHI, and I have been a fine artist since my youth. I find inspiration from the innovative styles of contemporary and urban art,such as graphic and graffiti illustrations. I strongly believe that art is a global form of communication. It educates visually by either moving you, pleasing you, or even inspiring you.
